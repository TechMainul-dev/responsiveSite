China launches national cloud learning platform as teaching goes online amid epidemic

China launched a national cloud learning platform on Monday for millions of students who are restricted to their homes over safety concerns arising from the fight against the novel coronavirus outbreak. As of Wednesday, the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) had killed 2,007 people and infected more than 74,000 in the country. As part of nationwide epidemic control, the authorities postponed the start of the new school semester that was supposed to begin on February 17. A new resumption date has yet to be confirmed.
